Welcome to the Goldfish Bowl

A site celebrating the hobby of the ´Aquarist´ which is the keeping of fish in an aquarium. So don´t be shy, come on in and have a swim around.

Whether you are new to the hobby or a seasoned sea-dog of the aquarium world, you should find something to interest you on the site.
